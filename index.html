About

My mission in life is to cultivate a loving home for my children to become productive, caring individuals in society who are also grounded with strong familial values. I strive to be a truly altruistic individual and better than who I was yesterday. I believe that people are naturally benevolent and there are always circumstances that render them otherwise, therefore everyone deserves an opportunity to recalibrate themselves. My previous work experience incorporated a great deal of managerial tasks including management of time, people, and administrative obligations. In the past, I worked on a variety of teams that had various personality traits. I have managed to survive the volatile teams and always contributed to every team. Although, I work better with alpha-types, I prefer to work with a team of driven and flexible people who are not only team-oriented (helpful to others), but also focused on task completion. In my experience, this is best accomplished with only one clear alpha. I have a fondness of technology (hardware in particular) and the process of actualizing work efficiency. My passion is in music, art, and design. I enjoy praising/critiquing industrial design and contemplating how to revitalize the design.
